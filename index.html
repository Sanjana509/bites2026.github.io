Welcome

The program committee is delighted to invite you to BITES 2026, a premier forum at the intersection of blockchain technologies, Internet of Things, and sustainable energy systems.

The workshop will be held on 1–5 June 2026 in conjunction with IEEE ICBC 2026 in Brisbane, Australia, bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts.

Aim & Scope

Over the last two decades, network technologies and mobile data traffic have evolved rapidly, with global mobile data projected to continue growing significantly, driven by pervasive devices, high-speed connectivity, and data-intensive services.

While 5G deployments deliver higher data rates and improved throughput, open challenges in mobile edge computing, open and smart RAN, virtualization, and embedded AI continue to motivate innovative architectures and mechanisms.

BITES 2026 aims to provide a focused venue to discuss how blockchain, IoT, and sustainable energy technologies can jointly enable trustworthy, energy-aware, and scalable next-generation mobile Internet systems.
